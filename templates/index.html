How to Use the MRP System

A step-by-step guide to get you started

Getting Started:

  • Click on the "Signin" button in the navigation menu
  • Fill in your username, email, and password
  • Once registered, you can log in to access all features
  • Your profile will be saved for future visits

For Crop Farming:

  • Navigate to the "BOM" section from the menu
  • Enter the number of hectares you plan to farm
  • Select your desired crop from the dropdown menu
  • Click "Calculate Crops" to see total seedlings, costs, and required inputs
  • View recommendations for fertilizers, pesticides, and weedicides

For Livestock Farming:

  • Select the animal type from the livestock calculator
  • Enter the number of animals you want to raise
  • Click "Calculate Livestock" to get housing, feed, and vaccination requirements
  • Review estimated costs and planning details

Finding Products:

  • Click on "Agro Products" in the navigation menu
  • Browse through available agricultural products
  • View detailed information including prices and specifications
  • Use the search feature or voice search to find specific products

Adding Products (for sellers):

  • Navigate to "Add Products" section
  • Fill in product details, pricing, and specifications
  • Submit to make your products available to the community

Participating in Discussions:

  • Go to "Community Forum" from the navigation
  • Read existing discussions and comments from other farmers
  • Click "Add Discussion" to start a new topic
  • Share your experiences, ask questions, and get advice
  • Comment on posts to engage with the community

Voice-Activated Search:

  • Look for the "Voice Search" button in the navigation bar
  • Click the button and speak your search query
  • The system will automatically search for products matching your voice command
  • Note: Voice search works best in Chrome and other modern browsers

About Us

Learn more about our mission and values

Our Mission

To revolutionize agricultural planning in Africa by providing farmers with cutting-edge Material Requirement Planning tools that simplify complex farming decisions. We are committed to bridging the technology gap in agriculture, ensuring that both small-scale and commercial farmers have access to accurate resource calculations, cost estimates, and agricultural insights. Through our platform, we aim to reduce waste, optimize resource allocation, increase crop yields, and improve livestock management, ultimately contributing to food security and sustainable farming practices across the continent.

Our Vision

To become Africa's premier digital agriculture platform, transforming how farmers plan, manage, and scale their agricultural operations. We envision a future where every farmer, regardless of their location or farm size, has access to professional-grade planning tools, real-time market information, and a supportive community of agricultural experts. By 2030, we aim to empower over 1 million farmers across Africa, contributing to a 40% increase in agricultural productivity, fostering innovation in agribusiness, and creating sustainable livelihoods that drive economic growth in rural communities while preserving our environment for future generations.
